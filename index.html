在城市中行驶时，您有机会成为“日常生活手册”的艺术民工，穿越时间和空间工作。正如手册所述，这个世界只是覆盖在根状宇宙上的一层薄膜。通过这个应用程序，您可以访问这些通常隐藏的工作地点，帮助宇宙的发展。

将相机对准窗外，并点击“获取地点”按钮，您将获得一个上海的位置及其在根状宇宙中的对应地点。系统将为您生成一张 AI 图片。您可以多次点击“获取地点”按钮，探索不同的地点。

As you ride through the city, you have the opportunity to become part of the art workers of "The Manual of Everyday Life" as we work across time and space. As the manual tells us, this world is only one layer of film stretched across the rhizomatic universe. With this app, you can visit these usually hidden worksites and help the development of the universe.

Aim the camera out the window and click the "Get Site" button to receive a location in Shanghai and its mapping to a site in the rhizomatic universe. An AI-generated image will be created for you. You can click the "Get Site" button as many times as you like to explore different locations.
